Things I Am Currently Thinking About

  • LLMs As the ability of LLMs expand and the truly novel things they are accomplishing are publicized seemingly each new day (see my links on my blog for this month), I am forced to update my priors about how and how often I should be incorporating them in my own life. I have found LLMs helpful for a variety of tasks, but I think that it is necessary to put personal limits on their use case for a number of reasons -- privacy, pedagogy, mission critical paths. I am going reflect on this more over the upcoming month.
  • Pricing Fair Odds (2x) I haven't had as much time to work on this as I would have liked, so continuing on here! I have been on a deep dive about how to assess the fair odds of events, specifically with regard to sports betting. I am working on a blog post on this subject and have been independently researching how to better price correlation. Pricing correlation is very important for the massively popular bet type "Same Game Parlays". A good method of doing this for certain markets could lead to a nice edge.
