Things I Am Currently Thinking About

  • Social Media and the Oral Tradition Derek Thompson's podcast with Joe Weisenthal put into a clearer lens something I have long sniffed, but couldn't make much sense of as a whole. It's trite to point out that social media is killing our attention spans, making us less social, spreading disinformation, etc. However, through orality theory Joe lays out how social media is changing the way we share, consume, and digest information.
