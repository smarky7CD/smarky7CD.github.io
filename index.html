Things I Am Currently Thinking About

  • Retail Trading A project I have been working on at work over the past month has been about tagging retail flow on the trading tape. I have come to build a strong understanding of the market micro-structure involving individual investors. A blog post about this will soon be available on my company blog.
  • Pricing Fair Odds I have been on a deep dive about how to assess the fair odds of events, specifically with regard to sports betting. I am working on a blog post on this subject and have been independently researching how to better price correlation. Pricing correlation is very important for the massively popular bet type "Same Game Parlays". A good method of doing this for certain markets could lead to a nice edge.
