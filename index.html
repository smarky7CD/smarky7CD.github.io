Things I Am Currently Thinking About

  • Optimization Function How does one select a proper optimization function when analyzing trade-offs in policy proposals? Unconstrained utilitarianism-esque seems unrealistic and perhaps incoherent, while uber-individualistic functions also seem equally unappealing. What is an acceptable happy medium for building a just and flourishing society?
  • Reality-aligned Governance (x3) What would it look like to build policy frameworks that optimize for empirical outcomes and scale for the variation in constituents' abilities, motivations, and risk tolerance? I will write on this soon.
